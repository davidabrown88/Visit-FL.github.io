Orlando, Florida

Orlando is primarily known for being a major tourist destination, with world-famous theme parks such as Walt Disney World Resort, Universal Orlando Resort, and SeaWorld Orlando. Here are some key features of Orlando:

  • Population: 287,442
  • Year Incorporated: 1885
  • Region: Central Florida
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $58,720 (vs. state average $57,350)
