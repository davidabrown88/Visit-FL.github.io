Tallahassee, Florida

Tallahassee is the capital city of Florida, known for its rich history, Southern charm, and natural beauty. Here are some key features of Tallahassee:

  • Population: 194,500
  • Year Incorporated: 1824
  • Region: North Florida
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $62,530 (vs. state average $57,350)
