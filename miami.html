Miami, Florida

Miami is a vibrant and multicultural city known for its beautiful beaches, lively nightlife, and diverse cultural scene. Here are some key features of Miami:

  • Population: 463,347
  • Year Incorporated: 1896
  • Region: Southeast Florida
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $59,860 (vs. state average $57,350)
